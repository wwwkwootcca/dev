# {{page.title}}:
{:.no_toc}
- - -
K-W OOTC is an evening<sup>*</sup> program, running from November into April.

_Although most volunteer time occurs during program hours, many activities occur outside of those hours.
<br><sup>*</sup> St. Matthew's also offers Breakfast Thursday mornings._

Please see the
[_Pocket Card_](//communityservices.regionofwaterloo.ca/en/housing/resources/docs_admin-2244399-v1-pocket_card_fall_2016-accessible.pdf "Region of Waterloo"){:target="_blank"}
and other links under _Guest Help_ for additional program details.


<br>Being a loose federation of associating organizations, each location runs independently in its own, albeit similar, fashion.
